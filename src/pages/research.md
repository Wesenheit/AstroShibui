Most of my research is centered around the topic of Bayesian inference
applied to physical problems. My interests:

1. Simulation-based inference
2. Probabilistic machine learning — diffusion, normalizing flows, VAEs, etc
3. Deep generative priors
4. Probabilistic volumetric rendering

I am also deeply interested in High-Performance Computing (HPC) and
the general art of scaling up models/simulations.

In my previous life I was a researcher in the field of observational astrophysics
(gravitational microlensing) and theoretical high energy astrophysics (plasma physics,
physics of accretion, jets).
